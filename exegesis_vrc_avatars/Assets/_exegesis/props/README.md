# Screwdriver
Use to remove and reinstall panels, as well as some other capabilities. The screwdriver has a contact sender tagged `screwdriver`.

The evil screwdriver features SPS.

## Requirements
- [Poiyomi 9.3](https://www.poiyomi.com/download/#method-2-alcom--creator-companion)
- [VRCFury 1.1279.0](https://vrcfury.com/download/)

## Installation
1. Download the package and drag into your unity *Project* tab
2. From the *Project* tab, Drag your screwdriver of choice into the *Scene* tab
3. In the *Scene* tab, move the screwdriver into the palm of your avatar's right hand
4. In the *Heirarchy* tab, drag the screwdriver into your avatar

## Usage
The screwdriver creates a sub-menu in your expressions (radial) menu called `Tools/`. Once enabled, you can make the `fist` gesture with your right hand to enable the `screwdriver` contact sender, which removes panels on Exegesis avatars. (The contact sender becomes disabled if the SPS plug is enabled.)
